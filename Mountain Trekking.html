Top 10 Mountain Trekking Spots

Rank Location Country Specialty Starting At
1 Everest Base Camp Trek Nepal Offers stunning views of Mt. Everest, cultural encounters with Sherpas, and iconic Himalayan landscapes. 10,999
2 Inca Trail to Machu Picchu Peru Ancient stone pathways through the Andes, leading to the lost city of Machu Picchu — a mix of nature and archaeology. 10,999
3 Kilimanjaro Trek Tanzania Highest free-standing mountain in the world; trek through multiple ecosystems from rainforest to arctic summit. 10,999
4 Tour du Mont Blanc France, Italy, Switzerland A circular route around Mont Blanc, crossing three countries with breathtaking alpine views and charming villages. 10,999
5 Annapurna Circuit Trek Nepal Diverse landscapes — from tropical valleys to high mountain passes — and rich cultural immersion. 10,999
6 Patagonia’s Torres del Paine Circuit Chile Famous for dramatic granite towers, glaciers, and pristine lakes; perfect for adventure and solitude seekers. 10,999
7 John Muir Trail USA (California) Passes through Yosemite and the High Sierra, known for its stunning wilderness and serene mountain lakes. 10,999
8 Laugavegur Trail Iceland Unique volcanic terrain, colorful rhyolite mountains, hot springs, and glacial rivers. 10,999
9 Drakensberg Grand Traverse South Africa Offers rugged cliffs, remote plateaus, and panoramic views of Africa’s highest mountain range south of Kilimanjaro. 10,999
10 Chadar Trek (Frozen River Trek) India A winter trek over the frozen Zanskar River in Ladakh — famous for its ice trail, caves, and Himalayan beauty. 10,999
