Top 10 Sky Diving Spots

Rank Location Country Specialty Starting At
1 Interlaken Switzerland Skydive over the Swiss Alps, with breathtaking views of Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau mountains and turquoise lakes below. 30,999
2 Dubai Palm Drop Zone UAE Experience a tandem jump over Palm Jumeirah, offering a surreal aerial view of the man-made island and Dubai skyline. 30,999
3 Fox Glacier New Zealand Jump over glaciers, mountains, and rainforests — all within one view; one of the most scenic jumps on Earth. 30,999
4 Hawaii (Oahu Island) USA Freefall over the Pacific Ocean, lush valleys, and volcanic mountains — tropical skydiving paradise. 30,999
5 Mount Everest (Sagarmatha National Park) Nepal Highest skydiving drop zone in the world, with a view of Mount Everest — the ultimate adventure challenge. 30,999
6 Cape Town South Africa Skydive with a stunning view of Table Mountain and the Atlantic Ocean, often with whales visible below. 30,999
7 Cairns Australia Jump over the Great Barrier Reef, the only place where you can see coral reefs and rainforest from the air. 30,999
8 Denarau Island (Fiji) Fiji Known for island-hopping aerial views, turquoise seas, and tropical beaches. Ideal for first-time jumpers. 30,999
9 Grand Canyon USA Experience freefall over the iconic red rock canyons — a truly cinematic skydiving adventure. 30,999
10 Mysuru (Mysore) India India’s top skydiving destination; jump over the Chamundi Hills and lush Karnataka plains. Known for its safe operations, panoramic views, and affordable tandem jumps. 30,999
