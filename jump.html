Top 10 Rock Climbing Spots

Rank Location Country Specialty Starting At
1 Macau Tower Macau, China 233 m (764 ft) World’s highest commercial bungee jump; jumpers experience a 6-second free fall with panoramic city views. 20,999
2 Verzasca Dam (Contra Dam) Ticino, Switzerland 220 m (721 ft) Featured in James Bond’s GoldenEye; famous for its dramatic night jumps. 20,999
3 Bloukrans Bridge Western Cape, South Africa 216 m (709 ft) Highest bridge bungee in the world; stunning view over Bloukrans River valley. 20,999
4 Nevis Highwire Queenstown, New Zealand 134 m (440 ft) One of the most thrilling jumps; suspended from a cable in a canyon. 20,999
5 Europabrücke Bridge Innsbruck, Austria 192 m (630 ft) Offers breathtaking Alpine views; Europe’s most scenic bungee site. 20,999
6 The Last Resort Bridge Bhote Kosi River, Nepal 160 m (525 ft) Surrounded by lush forest and river gorge; serene yet heart-pounding. 20,999
7 Kawarau Bridge Queenstown, New Zealand 43 m (141 ft) World’s first commercial bungee site (opened 1988); birthplace of bungee jumping. 20,999
8 Niouc Bridge Val d’Anniviers, Switzerland 190 m (623 ft) Known as “Spider Bridge”; offers bungee, swing, and zip-line combo experiences. 20,999
9 Victoria Falls Bridge Border of Zambia & Zimbabwe 111 m (364 ft) Jump over Zambezi River with Victoria Falls mist creating a magical experience. 20,999
10 Rishikesh, Uttarakhand India 83 m (273 ft) India’s highest fixed platform bungee; surrounded by Himalayan foothills and Ganga River views. Operated by experts trained in New Zealand. 20,999
